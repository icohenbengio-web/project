The Problem
Modern families lack a shared, context-aware AI that understands their household's people, schedules, and routines — leaving co-parents to carry the mental load alone.

General-purpose AI has broad world knowledge but zero awareness of a family's personal context — names, routines, hobbies, upcoming events.

Coordination ends up scattered across sticky notes, chat logs, and forgotten reminders, with no shared "single source of truth."

Madeleine is the shared intelligence and extra administrative brain every modern family needs — secure, collaborative, and proactive.

Secure Collaborative Proactive LLM-powered
What it does

Unified household intelligence

🗓️

Synchronized Co-Parenting

Both partners share the exact same contextual ecosystem. Manage schedules, tasks, and reminders together in real-time — no more constant alignment overhead.

🔮

Proactive Anticipation

Madeleine cross-references birthdays, travel schedules, and routines to surface suggestions before you even feel the stress — gift ideas, packing lists, logistics.

🧠

Family Memory

Unlike generic AI tools, Madeleine safely remembers names, hobbies, allergies, and habits — anchoring powerful LLM intelligence in your private family ecosystem.

How it works

Simple to use,
smart by design

01

Set up your family profile

Add your family members, their birthdays, hobbies, allergies, and routines. Madeleine keeps this context private and secure.

02

Invite your co-parent

Share the same contextual space with your partner. Both of you see the same calendar, tasks, and AI memory — no more misaligned reminders.

03

Let Madeleine work for you

Ask in plain language. Get smart answers, proactive suggestions, and an AI that knows your family — not just the world.

Business Model

The "Family Organizer" subscription

💳

Single Family Plan

One subscription covers both parents + kids — €7.99/month, or €59.99/year (~€5/month). A 14–30 day free trial lowers the barrier to entry.

🎯

Target Market

~123,000 affluent, tech-savvy executive parents in Paris — about 6% of the Parisian population, based on Insee demographic data.

📈

Pricing Logic

Aligned with premium productivity app standards — high enough to cover LLM API and server costs, affordable enough for parents to justify.
